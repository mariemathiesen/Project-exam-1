EOBASIC HOME CHARGER


The EO Basic charger is designed for charging up at home, work, property developments and destinations. With no software, just plug the cable into your car and instantly get a charge. No nonsense. Just simple plug and play charging solution. Can be fittet with optional activation key.


– Charges every EV on the market today.

– Built in DC-RCM module, no need to extra Type B RCD.

– Advanced socket with auto-lock.

– Unique installation system for quick deployment and future upgrades (see video below).

– Power Ratings: 3.6kW- 7.2kW (Single Phase) + 11kW – 22kW (400v 3 Phase).

– 5 year product warranty.
