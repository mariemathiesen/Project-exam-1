ENERGY STORAGE


Nissan’s xStorage Home has been designed to be the most reliable and affordable home energy storage system available.


xStorage Home is housed in a single stylish unit, that integrates everything needed to manage and store energy in your home.


Nissan/Eaton provide energy storage systems for homes, businesses, manufacturing plants. Scaling up to the top of the energy tree, we also offer energy storage to suppliers at the grid level seeking greater stability.


Why install Nissan xStorage?

1 – Lower your electricity bills.

2 – Lower your CO2 footprint.

3 – Make a positive contribution to the environment.

4 – No more lights out. xStorage acts as a back-up power source.

5 – Easy to install and use.

6 – Safe technology.

7 – Customer service.


The system is supported by a network of over 1,000 distributors, working with qualified installers.
