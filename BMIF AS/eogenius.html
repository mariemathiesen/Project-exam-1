EOGENIUS & EOHUB






The eoGenius charger when coupled with eoHUB, makes Genius supersmart. Genius is designed for charging at home, work, on the go and at destinations (i.e. hotels and car parks). eoHUB comes connected to eoCloud and eoApp.


– Manage access to your chargers from eoCloud, up to 29 chargers to 1 eoHub (scaleable).

– Built in DC-RCM module, no need to extra Type B RCD.

– Dynamic power balancing.

– Control from your smartphone (eoAPP).

– Control via RFID.

– Billing, access and load management facilities.

– Over-the-air remote software upgrades.

– Chargers every EV on the market.

– Unique installation system for quick deployment and future upgrades (see video below).

– Power Ratings: 3.6kW- 7.2kW (Single Phase) + 11kW – 22kW (400v 3 Phase).

– 5 year product warranty.
