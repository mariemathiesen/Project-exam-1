ELVI


Charging your car has never been easier. Elvi has everything you need from wifi, apps features, load balancing, reading of Kw usage + much more.


Elvi has built-in DC leakage current detection, no need for a seperate type-B RCD.

Capacity: – 3.7kW, 7.4kW, 11kW, 22kW.

Amperage: – 16A or 32A (1- or 3-phase)

Connector: – An attached type 1 or type 2 cable

Attachment: Wall mounted.

Cable length: – 4 or 8 meters.

Dimensions: – 328 x 186 x 161 mm (LxWxH)

Connection: – Bluetooth 4.0, Dual Band WiFi 2.7/5 GHz, optional UMTS

Colors: – Misty Black / Forrest Green / Polar White
